DECENTRALIZED & ANONYMOUS
The Bitmessage network is used for posts and buyer/seller communication and the Bitcoin network is used for payments. The app connects to those networks using the location hiding Tor network.
TWO PARTY ESCROW
Buyer and seller lock mutual security deposits and payment into a Bitcoin transaction that can only be released when both agree on payment or refund. This keeps incentives aligned without the need for escrow agents or reputation systems.
FREE, OPEN & UNCENSORED
Free client, no fees, no altcoin. Open source code available on Github under an MIT license. All posts are directly to the Bitmessage network and therefore cannot be censored.
