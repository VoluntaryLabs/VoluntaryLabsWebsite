Voluntary
NET
VoluntaryNet is an in-browser decentralized (not federated) platform owned and run by the users. The aim is to give users complete control over their digital lives.
WORKING
  • Create and manage multiple identities and contacts
  • Private direct messaging (e.g. SMS, email, direct messages)
  • Public posting (e.g. twitter / FB feed posts)
  • Feed of public posts of contacts
IN PROGRESS
  • Embedded images in posts
  • Public post replies
  • Public reposts
  • Public post likes
PLANNED
  • Group chat
  • Public shared channels
  • Image, Audio, Video feeds
  • Audio / Video Chat
  • Onion Routing for Privacy
  • Integrated cryptocurrency wallets
  • P2P Ecommerce
